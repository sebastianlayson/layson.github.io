Reflection and Learnings about the course.

To be honest, I never thought I would ever see the end of Managing Enterprise because of all the hardships that me and my classmates were greeted with throughout this course, but we did it. This final project is a statement that all of my hardwork has been recognized. I would like to say that all of the knowledge I gained from this course has been a crucial part of making me the man that I am today. If resources allow, I will continue learning about new automations, network capabilities and all the services. If I had the time and space to make an automated network, I would make my very own. From this course the most helpful skill I have acquired is critical thinking, this skill is very pivotal in every situation we face in our daily lives, we use this skill to make important decisions, to make correct decisions. Without a doubt in my mind, this course has been extremely helpful.

My Experience.

My experience in this course has been exciting, this course was my first face to face classes after two years of being in online classes. I had expected to have trouble readjusting to the whole set up, but with a very welcoming professor, and approachable, friendly, classmates, it became easy. If I was having trouble with an activity, I knew I could ask my classmates or professor and they would help me. The activities in this course were easy to understand but relatively hard to perform, which caused me to put my critical thinking skill in to test. All in all it was a fun experience with both learnings and hardships.
